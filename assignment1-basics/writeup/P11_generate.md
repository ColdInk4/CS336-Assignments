## Problem (generate): Generate text (1 point)

### Prompt

Using your decoder and your trained checkpoint, report the text generated by your model. You may need to manipulate decoder parameters, `temperature`, `top-p`, and so on, to get fluent outputs.

> Deliverable: Text dump of at least `256` tokens of text, or until the first `<|endoftext|>` token, and a brief comment on the fluency of this output and at least two factors which affect how good or bad this output is.

### Answer

Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Lily. She had a big, red ball. Lily loved to play with her ball every day. One day, she saw a small, helpless bird on the ground. The bird could not fly.
Lily wanted to help the bird. She thought of a plan. She would use her big, red ball to help the bird. Lily threw the ball up in the air, but the bird did not fly. The bird was sad.
Then, something unexpected happened. The bird started to grow bigger and bigger! It was not a bird at all, but a big, shiny balloon! The balloon was magic. It could fly and make wishes come true. Lily was so happy. She played with her new balloon all day long.

Tim started to clean his room. He put away his toys and made his bed. But then, something unexpected happened. Tim's toy car started to move on

生成结果整体比较流畅，风格很接近 TinyStories：词汇简单，句子较短，并且有比较清楚的故事结构。模型生成了一个基本完整的情节，包括 Lily 发现小鸟、尝试帮助它、最后出现魔法气球的结尾。不过输出也有一些问题，例如 “big, red ball” 和 “the bird could not fly” 这类表达有重复，且“小鸟变成魔法气球”的转折比较突然，逻辑上略显生硬。

影响生成质量的因素至少有两个。第一是采样参数，例如 temperature 和 top-p；较低的 temperature 会让输出更稳定、更少语法错误，但也可能让故事更保守、更重复。第二是 checkpoint 本身的训练质量，例如训练 token 数、validation loss 和数据/tokenization 质量；训练更充分、validation loss 更低、tokenization 更干净的模型通常会生成更连贯、异常更少的文本。

在生成时我还发现，早期序列化 token ids 的流程没有把 `<|endoftext|>` 编码成 special token，而是把它当作普通字符串拆开了。因此对于这个 checkpoint，我在展示结果时按照第一次出现的 `<|endoftext|>` 字符串进行截断；后续我已经修复了 tokenization 流程。
